Delivery of goods is carried out to the entrance of an apartment building, or to the gate of a private residential building, if a vehicle can enter. Otherwise, the transfer of the goods can be carried out in a place that is maximum possible for the passage and parking of the vehicle to the delivery address. The unloading of the goods from the vehicle is carried out by the buyer. If it is impossible to drive up to the place of unloading, manual bringing of furniture is possible, but only by agreement with the employee delivering the goods. Assembly in the cities of presence and those equated to them is carried out after the delivery of furniture, not earlier than the next working day by agreement.

Guarantee is responsible for the compliance of the goods with the requirements, provided that the consumer observes the rules for assembling and caring for furniture. Claims from the buyer are accepted by the store that sold the goods within 18 months from the date of purchase of the furniture, upon presentation of documents confirming the fact of purchase: a contract, a cash register receipt or a receipt for a cash receipt, a warranty card.

Quality materials used in the manufacture of furniture are subject to incoming control for compliance with regulatory and technical documents in force for a given period in the industry. Specifically controlled indicators and methods of their control are specified in these documents. For example, wood-based materials are controlled for size, grade, moisture, warpage, adhesive materials - for appearance, dry residue, viscosity, adhesive ability, adhesive strength, etc.
